USA Shootings from 2018 - 2022
Throughout 2018 till 2022, Texas remains the top state in terms of mass shooting casualties. This is more than California despite having only 2/3 of California's population.

Texas and California have consistently been the states with the most gun deaths throughout 2018 until 2022.

Both states maintained a death count of over 30 for the past 5 years with the exception of Texas which had 29 deaths in 2018.

When sorting by states, we can see that most states tend to have an upwards trend in terms of deaths, which suggests that the USA is becoming less and less peaceful throughout the years.
